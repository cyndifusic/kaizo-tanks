About Install Guide Updates Support

Welcome! Looks like you've stumbled upon my Wii Play Tanks level pack, Kaizo Tanks. It's a work-in-progress and there are currently no public releases, so check the Updates page for progress reports on the project.

The pack will have 20 levels of varying difficulties, featuring humorous twists and fun level mechanics all throughout. The best part? If you have a Wii or Wii U, a copy of Wii Play, a computer, an Internet connection, and an SD card, you'll be able to play it yourself!
