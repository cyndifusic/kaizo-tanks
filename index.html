Kaizo Tanks

About Install Guide Updates Support

Welcome! I'm cyndifusic. Looks like you've stumbled upon my Wii Play Tanks level pack, Kaizo Tanks. If you're just here to download it, head on over to the Install Guide.

The pack will have 20 levels of varying difficulties, featuring humorous twists and fun level mechanics all throughout. The best part? If you have a Wii or Wii U, a copy of Wii Play, a computer, an Internet connection, and an SD card, you can play it yourself!
